What is Steam Roulette?

Steam Roulette is a series where we try out a bunch of viewer-suggested games. The catch is that we don't pick the games we play...

Each suggested game is assigned a number on a roulette wheel. Whatever number the ball lands on is the game that gets played.

This site exists simultaneously as an archive of previously played Steam Roulette games, and as a repository of game suggestions for future Steam Roulette streams.
